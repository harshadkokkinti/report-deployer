Finding 01

Spoiled clam chowder incident left unanswered

From the review
“I really wanted to like this place. I understand they were short-staffed on a weekday, and I tried to be patient with that. Unfortunately, our experience was disappointing. We were served clam chowder that appeared to be spoiled. The taste and smell were clearly off, so we flagged down one of the servers. There seemed to be a language barrier, so it took some time to explain that the issue wasn't just that we didn't like it - it seemed spoiled. We then informed our server, Max. His reaction made it seem like this may not have been the first time something like this happened, which was concerning. After seeing other reviews mention undercooked food and food quality issues, it really makes me wonder if proper quality checks are lacking here. Overall, I felt the food quality did not meet the prices they were charging. I just didn't see the value. What you're really paying for is the view and the vibe, not necessarily the food. The location has potential, but serving food that appears spoiled is a serious issue, and I hope management takes food safety and quality control more seriously.”
