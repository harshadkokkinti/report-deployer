“This hotel is, without exaggeration, one of the most iconic and luxurious hotels in Las Vegas. From the moment you step inside, everything is designed to impress: Renaissance-style painted ceilings, marble everywhere, indoor and outdoor canals with their famous gondolas, luxury shops, top-notch restaurants, and a gigantic casino that never sleeps. The gondola experience is probably the hotel's most distinctive feature. Cruising along the canals while the gondoliers sing creates an atmosphere quite different from the typical Vegas casino ambiance. It might seem a bit touristy, but that's precisely what makes it entertaining and memorable. The entire setting manages to transport you to an extravagant version of Venice, with Italian piazzas, sculptures, frescoes, and an impressive level of detail. That said, the price reflects the level of luxury. It's not a budget hotel, and during peak season it can be quite expensive, especially after the Sphere boom and the high demand in the area. But if you're looking for luxury, spaciousness, a prime location on the Strip, and a truly complete experience, the Venetian delivers. In addition to the hotel itself, you have access to renowned restaurants, spectacular pools, upscale shopping, beautifully renovated suites, and direct access to virtually everything important on the Strip. It's one of those hotels where you could easily spend several days without ever needing to leave.”
